39-310
Experiential Learning III
0 Units
The engineer of the 21st century will need to operate effectively in many settings and often with a global perspective. Being curious and constantly looking for inspiration are critical for lifelong learning. This course, designed for all CIT juniors, requires the student to choose and experience activities for development and growth that are not part of formal course work. Acceptable experiences are listed in the course syllabus on Canvas.
Prerequisites: 39-220
