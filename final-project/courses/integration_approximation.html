21-122
Integration, Differential Equations & Approximation
10 Units
Integration by trigonometric substitution and partial fractions; arclength; improper integrals; Simpson’s and Trapezoidal Rules for numerical integration; separable differential equations, Newton’s method, Euler’s method, Taylor’s Theorem including a discussion of the remainder, sequences, series, power series, parametric curves, polar coordinates, vectors, dot product.
Prerequisites: 21-112 or 21-120
