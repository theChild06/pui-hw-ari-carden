24-231
Fluid Mechanics
10 Units
Hydrostatics. Control volume concepts of mass, momentum, and energy conservation. Eulers and Bernoullis equations. Viscous flow equations. Head loss in ducts and piping systems. Dimensional analysis and similitude as an engineering tool. Measurement techniques.
Prerequisites: (33-141 or 33-106 or 33-151) and 21-122
