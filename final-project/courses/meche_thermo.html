24-221
Thermodynamics
10 Units
Temperature and thermometry; equations of state for fluids and solids; work, heat, and the first law; internal energy, enthalpy, and specific heats; energy equations for flow; change of phase; the second law, reversibility, absolute temperature, and entropy; combined first and second laws; availability; power and refrigeration cycles. Applications to a wide range of processes and devices.
Prerequisites: (33-141 or 33-151 or 33-121 or 33-106) and 21-122 and 24-101
