12-212
Statics
9 Units
Introduction to vector mechanics; equivalent systems of forces; equilibrium of rigid bodies; free body diagram; distributed forces; hydrostatic forces, effective forces, centroids; applications to simple statically determine trusses, beams, frames, cables and other physical systems; friction.
Corequisites: 12-100 and 33-141 and 21-122
